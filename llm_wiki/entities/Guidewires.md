# Guidewires (亲水/斑马导丝)

## Overview
High-performance guidewires for GI, Urology, and Peripheral Vascular access. Featuring Zebra (striped) patterning for visual confirmation of movement and hydrophilic coating for ultra-low friction.

## Market Strategy
- **Target Brands**: [[Terumo]], [[Boston Scientific]].
- **High Intent Keywords**: `hydrophilic guidewire`, `terumo guide wire 0.035`, `boston scientific guidewires`.
- **CPC Range**: $2.50 - $3.70.
- **Strategy**: Stable volume-driver. Focus on `0.035"` core parameter for SEO. Position as the reliable, cost-effective alternative to market leaders.

## Regulatory Status
- **Priority**: High (Phase 1).
- **Reasoning**: Well-defined test standards (kink resistance, friction coefficient). Low clinical complexity for access devices.
- **Action**: Leverage [[Compliance_Framework]] for rapid market entry.

## Technical Specs
- **Core Material**: Nitinol (Shape Memory).
- **Coating**: Hydrophilic (PVP-based).
- **Tip**: Straight, J-tip, Angled.
- **Radiopacity**: Tungsten-loaded for fluoroscopic visibility.
