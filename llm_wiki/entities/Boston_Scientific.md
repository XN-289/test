# Boston Scientific

## Overview
A worldwide developer, manufacturer, and marketer of medical devices used in interventional medical specialties.

## Market Position
- **Dominant Products**: [[Jagwire]] (Guidewires), [[Resolution Clip]] (Hemoclips), [[SpyGlass]] (Endoscopy).
- **Strategy**: Strong presence in GI and interventional cardiology. Leader in high-end guidewire and clip technology.
