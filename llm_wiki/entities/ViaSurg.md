# ViaSurg

## Overview
ViaSurg is positioned as a "Global Medical Standard Traffic Arbitrage Machine" (全球医疗标品流量套利机器). The strategic goal is to leverage established industry standards (Ethicon, Medtronic, etc.) to capture B2B procurement traffic through optimized digital presence and competitive pricing.

## Strategic Pillars
- **Budget Allocation**: Focus on high-value "Gold Mine" keywords with high CPC (e.g., [[Hemoclips]], [[Biopsy Forceps]]).
- **SKU Targeting**: Create specific landing pages for cross-referenced (平替互译) parts (e.g., [[Ethicon B5LT]]).
- **Negative SEO**: Filter out non-B2B traffic (students, home users, veterinary).

## Regulatory Roadmap
- **Phase 1 ( 造血期)**: Prioritize low-barrier, high-volume products like [[Trocars]], [[Polymer Clips]], [[Guidewires]].
- **Phase 2 (暴利期)**: Follow up with high-margin items like [[Hemoclips]], [[Ultrasonic Shears]], [[Absorbable Sutures]].
- **Phase 3 (持久战)**: Long-term handling of highly regulated items like [[Staplers]], [[Hernia Meshes]].

## Supply Chain
- Partners: [[Hangzhou Sode]] (杭州索德微创), Suture & Stapler factories.
- Strategy: Utilize OBL (Own Brand Labeler) to leverage existing factory FDA/CE certifications.
