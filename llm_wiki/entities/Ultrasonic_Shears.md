# Ultrasonic Shears (超声刀头)

## Overview
Active energy device for simultaneous cutting and coagulation of tissue. Operates at ultrasonic frequencies (e.g., 55.5 kHz) to minimize thermal spread.

## Regulatory Status
- **Priority**: Medium (Phase 2).
- **Reasoning**: Active device (Energy Device). Requires IEC 60601-1 / IEC 60601-1-2 electrical safety and electromagnetic compatibility (EMC) testing.
- **Action**: Use Phase 1 revenue to fund rigorous EMC and bench testing.

## Technical Specs
- **Frequency**: 55.5 kHz.
- **Thermal Spread**: < 2.0 mm.
- **Vessel Sealing**: Reliable up to 5mm diameter.
- **Handpiece**: Reusable or Disposable variants available.
