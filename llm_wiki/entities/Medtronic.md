# Medtronic (Covidien)

## Overview
A global leader in medical technology, especially in surgery, patient monitoring, and cardiology. Acquired Covidien in 2015, strengthening its position in the MIS/Lap market.

## Market Position
- **Dominant Products**: [[Endo GIA]] (Staplers), [[Sonicision]] (Ultrasonic Shears), [[Ligasure]] (Vessel Sealing).
- **Strategy**: Strong presence in laparoscopic surgery. Market leader in surgical stapling alongside Ethicon.
