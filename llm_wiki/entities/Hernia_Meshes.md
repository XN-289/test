# Hernia Meshes (疝气修补网片)

## Overview
Surgical mesh for reinforcement of soft tissue defects in hernia repair. Primarily made from synthetic non-absorbable polymers (e.g., Polypropylene).

## Market Strategy
- **Target Brands**: [[Bard]] ([[BD]]), [[Ethicon]].
- **High Intent Keywords**: `polypropylene mesh`, `surgical mesh`.
- **CPC Range**: $3.59 - $10.00.
- **Strategy**: Material-first messaging. Avoid brand-heavy marketing; instead, emphasize "Pure Polypropylene Quality" and "Zero-additive" manufacturing.

## Regulatory Status
- **Priority**: Low (Phase 3).
- **Reasoning**: High-risk implantable. History of major litigation in the industry makes this a "Minefield" for new entrants.
- **Action**: Deprioritize. Monitor long-term safety data before aggressive entry.

## Technical Specs
- **Material**: Monofilament Polypropylene.
- **Weight**: Ultra-light / Light / Heavyweight options.
- **Pore Size**: Optimized for tissue ingrowth (e.g., Macroporous).
