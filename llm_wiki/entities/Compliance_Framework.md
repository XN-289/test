# Compliance Framework (Regulatory Rigor)

## Overview
ViaSurg operates under a strict "Verified Transparency" model for regulatory compliance, leveraging existing factory certifications while maintaining independent quality audits.

## Regulatory Pathways
- **OBL (Own Brand Labeler)**: Utilizing partner factory FDA 510(k) and CE MDR certificates to ensure rapid and legal market entry.
- **Relabeler/Repackager Registration**: ViaSurg is registered as a Relabeler/Repackager, assuming legal responsibility for branding and quality assurance as per FDA 21 CFR 807.

## Verification Protocols
- **ISO 13485**: Certified Quality Management System for medical devices.
- **Evidence Protocols**: Every product shipment includes a digital link to its specific quality verification report.
