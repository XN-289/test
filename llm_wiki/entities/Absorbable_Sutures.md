# Absorbable Sutures (可吸收缝线)

## Overview
Synthetic absorbable surgical sutures (e.g., PGA, PGLA). Designed to provide reliable wound support during critical healing and then absorb via hydrolysis.

## Regulatory Status
- **Priority**: Medium (Phase 2).
- **Reasoning**: Absorbable materials require time-dependent biodegradation studies (e.g., tensile strength retention over 14, 28, 42 days).
- **Action**: Use standard OBL route for Phase 1, followed by own-brand registration for premium suture variants.

## Technical Specs
- **Material**: Polyglycolic Acid (PGA) / Polyglactin 910 (PGLA).
- **Structure**: Braided / Monofilament.
- **Coating**: Polycaprolactone and Calcium Stearate.
- **Absorption Time**: ~60-90 days (complete).
