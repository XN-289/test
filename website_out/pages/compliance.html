ViaSurg operates under a strict "Verified Transparency" model for regulatory compliance, leveraging existing factory certifications while maintaining independent quality audits.

The OBL (Own Brand Labeler) strategy allows ViaSurg to enter the US and EU markets in weeks rather than years by utilizing partner factory FDA 510(k) and CE MDR certificates. This strategy accelerates market entry while maintaining 100% legal compliance through OEM-OBL agreements.
